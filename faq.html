FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. How do you rate the books?

Our ratings are based on a 5-star system considering plot, character development, and Gen Z relevance.

2. Are the external bookstore links safe?

Absolutely, as the site only provides links to reputable stores like Shopee Mall and Popular Bookstore.

3. Who is the target audience for Lumina?

Lumina is primarily designed for teenagers and young adults, specifically focusing on Generation Z (Gen Z).

4. Can I buy books directly from the Lumina website?

No, Lumina does not have online purchasing features; however, it provides external links to reputable stores to help you buy the books.

5. Do I need to create an account or log in?

No, the platform is designed to be informational and interactive without requiring a user login.

6. Is the website accessible on my phone?

Yes, the website is designed to be responsive and user-friendly for both smartphones and laptops.

7. What is the "Editor's Picks" section?

It is a curated selection of books highly recommended by our team for their exceptional quality and impact.

8. Can I search for books by specific genres only?

Yes, you can use the search bar or navigate through the 'Fiction' menus.
