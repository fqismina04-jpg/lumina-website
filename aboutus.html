ABOUT LUMINA

LUMINA is a book review-based corporate website developed to provide novel recommendations for Generation Z (Gen Z). The name is a combination of our team members' names: Nur, Haqmar, Ayu, and Qismina, representing light, moon, beauty, and jasmine.

OUR MISSION

To provide Gen Z with reliable and engaging novel reviews through an easy-to-use and visually appealing platform.

OUR VISION

To become a leading platform for reading inspiration, fostering creativity and critical thinking among Gen Z.
